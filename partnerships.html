VISION AVTR CONCEPT CAR

01:31
The first AVATAR 2 partnership to be announced was our global automotive partnership with Mercedes-Benz. This partnership kicked off in 2020 at CES showcasing the VISION AVTR, a concept car co-created by Mercedes-Benz and filmmakers to highlight the teams' shared values of sustainability, connectivity and Innovation.
